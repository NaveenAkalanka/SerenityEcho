# Atmosphere Videos

Place your animated video files in this folder with the following naming convention:

## Required Video Files

- `rain.mp4` or `rain.webm` - Rain animation
- `wind.mp4` or `wind.webm` - Wind animation
- `ocean.mp4` or `ocean.webm` - Ocean waves animation
- `forest.mp4` or `forest.webm` - Forest atmosphere
- `thunder.mp4` or `thunder.webm` - Thunder storm animation
- `fire.mp4` or `fire.webm` - Crackling fire animation
- `birds.mp4` or `birds.webm` - Birds flying/singing
- `night.mp4` or `night.webm` - Night crickets/starry sky
- `river.mp4` or `river.webm` - Flowing river
- `beach.mp4` or `beach.webm` - Beach waves
- `snow.mp4` or `snow.webm` - Falling snow
- `cafe.mp4` or `cafe.webm` - Coffee shop ambiance

## Video Specifications

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264) or WebM recommended
- **Resolution**: 720p or higher
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or 1:1
- **Duration**: 10-30 seconds (will loop)
- **Size**: Keep under 5MB per video for performance
- **Optimization**: Use low bitrate for smooth looping

## Notes

- Videos will auto-play, loop, and be muted
- They will be used as background overlays on the carousel cards
- Use smooth, calm animations that match the SerenityEcho aesthetic
